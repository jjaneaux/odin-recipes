Taco Salad

Description

A refreshing and tangy taco salad consisting of juicy ground beed and cool Thousand Island dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 head iceberg lettuce, chopped
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 block cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 bag tortilla chips
  • 1 jar (16 oz.) Thousand Island dressing

Directions

  1. Cook the ground beef.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl after being appropriately prepared.
  3. Mix thoroughly.
